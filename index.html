NEW LANGUAGE
VISUALS

Similar to the attitude of the talent, we will also be bolder and more modern visually.
To achieve this we will use many different means at our disposal: a faster edit, more modern light 

NEW LANGUAGE
OPENING

A great way to modernise the beginning of each film is to change the light setups.
For example, we start with our heroine striding through the grand, classical space. But at first there is only backlight. All the frontal lights are switched off. Our heroine is just seen as a cool elegant silhouette. For a short moment we can't yet recognise her.
It is a powerful, enigmatic entrance.
